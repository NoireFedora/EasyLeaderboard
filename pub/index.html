EasyLeaderboard.js

This is a javascript library for you to easily make a simple customized Leaderboard on your webiste.

You can define the Sorting of Leaderboard by yourself with your own function. You are also able to Add, Hide, Remove and Set links on any rows by simple functions. Adding and Removing ranking category (the way of sorting) and Setting maximum number of elements shown on a Leaderboard is also possible.

For details, please take a look on Examples and Documentation Sections.

I hope my library can make your programming life easier :)
